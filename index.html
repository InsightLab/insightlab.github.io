Welcome to Insight Data Science Lab

We build systems and algorithms to extract knowledge, find patterns, generate insights and predictions
from diverse data for various applications and visualization

Big Data Analysis

Big data analytics is the process of examining large, heterogeneous and varied data sets to uncover hidden patterns, unknown correlations, trends, preferences and other useful information that can help organizations make more-informed business decisions.

Large Scale Graph Processing

Massive graphs are nowadays used to represent many different kinds of real-world data, for instance communities and networks; processing efficiently these data structures represents a fundamental challenge in many domains.

Mobility Data Analysis

Mobility data represents a widely used term to indicate sources of information, possibly structured in many different ways. The importance of mobility data is going to increase even further in the future, given the ever growing diffusion of the internet of devices and the progressive introduction of the internet of things paradigm.

We are a research group at Federal University of Ceará (UFC) linked to the Department of Computing, which is accredited by Brazilian IT Law. It's composed by professors, researchers, collaborators and students of postgraduate and graduation. We conduct interdisciplinary research aimed at discovering the principles underlying the data science. The laboratory conducts research in many areas: cloud computing, machine learning, data visualization, semantic integration, social network analysis, trajectory exploration, time-dependent networks, text summarization and mining massive datasets.
